I don’t actually use my French press to make coffee. I just use it as a jug for my pour-over.

I always have coffee within an hour of waking up—it’s what really gets me going.

I’ve been drinking Trader Joe’s medium roast ground coffee for over a year now.

I picked up some Stumptown coffee beans in NYC, but since I don’t have a grinder, I used my Ninja Shark blender instead. It did a decent job, maybe a bit too fine.

I often forget I’m making coffee and end up pouring the water a little late when it’s already cooling down—but it still tastes good.
